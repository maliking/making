Types of Malware

What is Malware?

Malware is a piece of software that has been written in order to infect and manipulate a computer system. There are many different types of malware, and since you will most likely be affected by some in one way or another, it is important to learn the differences between them. This website will teach you the basics of the three most common types of malware.
